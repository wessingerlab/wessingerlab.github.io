The Wessinger Lab

The Wessinger Lab -- Plant Evolutionary Genetics

Hello! Welcome!

The Wessinger lab is located at the University of South Carolina Department of Biological Sciences. We are a collaborative and supportive group of researchers studying population genetic processes contributing to adaptive evolution. Our primary study system is Penstemon, an emerging model system for studying convergent / parallel evolution.
